# Google Interview Tips

### 1. Focus on Complexity & Trade-offs
For every coding problem, you must immediately state the Time and Space Complexity. Google interviewers care deeply about efficiency.
- Compare multiple approaches (e.g., Brute Force vs. Dynamic Programming).
- Explain *why* one is better in terms of memory vs CPU time.

### 2. Learn Googlyness
Google evaluates "Googlyness & Leadership" rounds.
- Show how you deal with ambiguity.
- Demonstrate that you are proactive and take initiative.
- Maintain humility and respect for team members.
