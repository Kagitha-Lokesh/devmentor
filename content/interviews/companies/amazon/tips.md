# Amazon Interview Tips

### 1. Master the 16 Leadership Principles (LPs)
Amazon's culture is deeply rooted in its Leadership Principles. Every interviewer will evaluate you against at least two or three LPs.
- **Customer Obsession**: Start with the customer and work backwards.
- **Ownership**: Never say "that's not my job."
- **Bias for Action**: Speed matters in business. Many decisions are reversible.

### 2. Format Answers in STAR
For all behavioral and HR questions, structure your answers strictly:
- **Situation**: Set the scene and context.
- **Task**: Explain the challenge and your specific responsibility.
- **Action**: Describe *what* you did, *how* you did it, and *why*.
- **Result**: Share quantified metrics (e.g., "reduced latency by 20%").
